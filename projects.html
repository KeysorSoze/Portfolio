Open Anxiety Tracker

Anxiety trackers are a therapist-recommended tool that can help people with mental health problems identify root causes of their issues. Finding a root cause of anxiety is the first step to healing. I find it immoral that anxiety tracker applications cost money for all the features. People should not feel like they must be nickel dimed because they want to be the best version of themself. This leads me to create my website with an anxiety tracker and other self-help tools. My target is anyone interested in self-help but cannot afford traditional methods. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
